## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: The app is not working or crashing. What should I do?**  
A: Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

**Q: The keyboard is not showing up in my apps. What should I do?**  
A: Go to iPhone Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Add New Keyboard, and make sure ArtKeyboard is enabled.

**Q: How do I enable Full Access for the keyboard?**  
A: Go to iPhone Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → ArtKeyboard, and toggle on "Allow Full Access".

**Q: The keyboard is slow or not loading properly.**  
A: Make sure your app is updated to the latest version from the App Store. Also check that Full Access is enabled in Settings.

**Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?**  
A: Email us at vgehani90@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your device model. Screenshots are helpful too!

**Q: How do I switch back to my default keyboard?**  
A: Tap and hold the globe icon 🌐 on your keyboard to switch between keyboards.
